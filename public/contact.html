Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days pan-India. Express (2–3 days) and Same-Day (Jodhpur only) options are also available at checkout. You'll receive a tracking number within 24 hours of placing your order.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy. Just WhatsApp us with your order number and reason. Products must be unworn and in original packaging. We'll arrange a pickup and process your refund or exchange within 5–7 days.
Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
Yes! COD is available pan-India with a small ₹15 handling charge. We also accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), credit/debit cards, and EMI on orders above ₹1,999.
