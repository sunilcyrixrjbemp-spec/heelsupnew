Orders
Order kaise place karein?
HeelsUp pe order karna bahut easy hai! Apni pasand ki product choose karo, size select karo, cart mein add karo, aur checkout karo. Hum UPI, cards, aur COD accept karte hain.
Order place karne ke baad cancel kar sakte hain?
Haan! Agar order shipped nahi hua hai, toh aap profile mein jaake cancel kar sakte hain. Shipped hone ke baad return policy apply hogi.
Order track kaise karein?
Order place hone ke baad aapko email/SMS mein tracking ID milegi. Profile > Orders mein bhi track kar sakte hain. Koi problem ho toh WhatsApp karo.
Mujhe order confirmation kab milega?
Order place karte hi aapko email aur SMS confirmation milega. Agar 30 minutes mein na mile, toh spam folder check karo ya hum se contact karo.
Bulk order possible hai?
Bilkul! Bulk orders ke liye special discounts milte hain. WhatsApp pe contact karo aur hum best price offer karenge.
Shipping & Delivery
Delivery charge kitna hai?
₹499 se upar ke orders pe FREE shipping milti hai. ₹499 se kam ke orders pe ₹49 shipping charge lagta hai.
Delivery kitne din mein hogi?
Jodhpur mein same-day ya next-day delivery milti hai. Baaki Rajasthan mein 2-3 din, India-wide 3-5 business days mein delivery hoti hai.
Kya aap India ke bahar deliver karte ho?
Abhi hum sirf India mein deliver karte hain. International shipping jald hi shuru hogi. Updates ke liye newsletter subscribe karo!
Delivery partner kaun hai?
Hum Blue Dart, Delhivery, aur India Post ke saath kaam karte hain best delivery experience ke liye.
Returns & Exchange
Return policy kya hai?
Delivery ke 7 din ke andar return ya exchange kar sakte hain. Product original condition mein, tags ke saath honi chahiye. Full return policy padhein.
Size galat ayi — kya exchange milegi?
Bilkul! Size exchange ke liye WhatsApp karo. Hum pickup arrange karenge aur sahi size bhej denge. Agar stock mein available ho toh 3-5 din mein exchange ho jaati hai.
Refund kab milega?
Return receive hone ke 5-7 business days mein refund original payment method pe aa jaata hai. UPI/Wallet refunds faster hoti hain.
Damaged product mila — kya karein?
Immediately WhatsApp pe photos bhejo. Hum 24 hours mein replacement arrange karenge. Damaged products ke liye koi extra charge nahi hoga.
Payments
Kaun se payment methods accept hote hain?
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, aur Cash on Delivery (COD) accept karte hain.
EMI available hai?
Haan! ₹1999 se upar ke orders pe EMI available hai major credit cards pe (3, 6, 12 months).
COD pe extra charge hai?
₹49 COD handling charge lagta hai. Prepaid orders pe yeh charge nahi hota.
Discount code kaise use karein?
Checkout page pe "Apply Coupon" field mein code daalo aur Apply button dabao. Discount automatically apply ho jaayega.
Products & Sizing
Size chart kahan hai?
Har product page pe size chart available hai. Generally India size 3-9 mein available hain. Doubt ho toh WhatsApp karo — hum guide karenge.
Products ki quality kaisi hai?
Har product quality-checked hai. Hum premium materials use karte hain. 10,000+ customers ne 4.8★ rating di hai — quality ki guarantee hai!
New arrivals kab aate hain?
Har hafte naye products aate hain! Instagram @heelsup.in follow karo ya newsletter subscribe karo — pehle notification aapko milega.
